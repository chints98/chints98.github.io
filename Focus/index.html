A reputation of excellence in healthcare

Since our establishment in February 2002, Focus imaging has built a strong reputation in the healthcare fraternity by providing world class diagnostic solutions to patients. We care for our patients by having a high level of service and attention.

The vision of the group is to provide excellent cost effective medical diagnostics to people from all walks of life, and fulfil the social obligation in a country like India where healthcare still has to reach the masses.

Our Services

We offer various services at our center, divided between radiological and pathological services.

Radiology

We are pioneers in the area of radiology, bringing India's first 3 Tesla Machine to patients, and offer state of the art services such as MRI, CT scans, X-ray and Ultrasound scans.

Pathology

Alongside radiology, we also have our own in-house pathology labs. Now patients can get tests done in house instead of running elsewhere to get tests done.
