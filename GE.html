    UX design and engineering intern · Summer 2017

    I spent this summer working on redesigning and prototyping core interactions for the CT suite of applications here at GE Healthcare. I used HTML/CSS and Angular, along with Sketch.

    As this was my first time working in an agile, cross-functional team of engineers and designers, I am extremely grateful to GE for giving me the opportunity to contribute.

    Some things I contributed to during my stint at GE include

    • Conducting user research along with a systems specialist to identify current pain points in the existing interfaces of both SVCT and Revolution CT product lines.
    • Prototyping various interactions and user testing the redesign of the existing Revolution product
    • Designing and prototyping core features for a new proof of concept application of the PET suite of SVCT applications.

    My work is currently pre-launch and covered by NDA, so I am unable to share it publicly. However, if you'd like to know more, do reach out to me
